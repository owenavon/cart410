Contrast Introduction

Contrast

Is Really Important

According to WCAG 2, contrast is a measure of the difference in perceived "luminance" or brightness between two colors. The brightness difference is expressed as a ratio ranging from 1:1 (white on white) to 21:1 (black on a white).

  • Pure red (#FF0000) has a ratio of 4:1. I am red text.
  • Pure green (#00FF00) has a very low ratio of 1.4:1. I am green text.
  • Pure blue (#000FF) has a contrast ratio of 8.6:1.I am blue text.

Contrast (Minimum)

In order to achieve Accessibility Level AA, The visual presentation of text and images of text has a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1

More info + images and examples to be added. https://webaim.org/articles/contrast/

Contrast (Enhanced)

In order to achieve Accessibility Level AAA, The visual presentation of text and images of text has a contrast ratio of at least 7:1

More info + images and examples to be added. https://webaim.org/articles/contrast/

Non-text Contrast

In order to achieve Accessibility Level AA, The visual presentation of Non-text Contrast has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1

More info + images and examples to be added. https://webaim.org/articles/contrast/
